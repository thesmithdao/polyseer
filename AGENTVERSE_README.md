# 🔮 Polyseer: Polymarket Oracle

**Ask anything about prediction markets in natural language and get a clear, calibrated answer.**

Polyseer turns [Polymarket](https://polymarket.com), the world's largest prediction market, into a simple conversation. Instead of digging through charts and order books, just ask. Polyseer reads the live markets and tells you the odds, the probability, and what's moving.

---

## What you can ask

**📊 Get the odds on anything**
> *"What are the odds the Fed cuts rates in July?"*

> *"Who will win the 2026 World Cup?"*

> *"What's trending on Polymarket?"*

Polyseer gives you a single, calibrated probability, anchored on what thousands of people are betting real money on right now plus a short, plain English reason.

**💰 Check a specific market**
> *"What's the price on the 2026 election market?"*

> *"Implied probability that ETH flips $5k?"*

**🔥 See what's hot**
> *"What are the trending markets right now?"*

> *"Show me the biggest markets today."*

**🔎 Find markets**
> *"Find markets about AI."*

> *"What prediction markets are there on sports?"*

---

## How to use it

Just **chat naturally**, no commands, no syntax. Ask a question the way you'd ask a friend, and Polyseer handles the rest: it finds the right markets, reads the live prices, and answers with the probability, the reasoning, and links to the markets it used.

## Grounded on crypto

For crypto questions, Polyseer also pulls the **live spot price** to ground the odds in reality, so instead of a bare number you get context:

> *"Bitcoin is at $63.7k; reaching $100k means +57% — the market prices that at ~25%."*

The Polymarket odds are always the answer; the live price just helps you read them.

## Why it's useful

Prediction markets are one of the most accurate real-time signals of what's likely to happen, because people are putting money behind their forecasts. Polyseer makes that signal instantly accessible to anyone (and to other AI agents), so you can check the odds on news, politics, crypto, economics, and more in seconds.

## Good to know

- Polyseer reads **live** Polymarket data, so answers reflect current market prices.
- It's **read-only** — it never trades, never touches your wallet, and never asks for funds.
- **Informational only — not financial advice.** Always do your own research.
